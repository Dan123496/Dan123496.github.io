About Me


I am Daniel Standring, a passionate programmer and software developer with over two years of experience in the gaming industry, specializing in Unity and C#.

During my career, I have successfully ported two PC games, PlateUp! and Cuisineer, to major console platforms, including Nintendo Switch, Xbox, and PlayStation. These projects deepened my expertise in platform-specific SDKs, development kits, and compliance requirements, allowing me to deliver optimized, high-quality experiences across diverse hardware.

I have also contributed to original IP development, working on gameplay mechanics, physics systems, and multiplayer functionality using frameworks like Photon Realtime, Photon Fusion, and Photon Quantum. One of my most rewarding challenges involved rebuilding a game in Photon Quantum, where I mastered deterministic physics systems and the Entity Component System (ECS) architecture.

At Huey Games, I made significant contributions to HueyCore, the company's proprietary porting software that simplifies game development for consoles. My most notable contributions included creating runtime and editor optimization tools, developing a customizable dev menu to enhance usability, and integrating native saving systems for various platforms. These tools have streamlined the optimization process for future projects, improving efficiency across the company.

In addition to my professional experience, I hold a First-Class Honours Degree in Computer Science from Manchester Metropolitan University an I am a Unity Certifed Associate Programmer. I have expanded my technical expertise through Unreal Engine C++ development courses and enjoy creating personal game projects, which you can explore here on my portfolio site.
